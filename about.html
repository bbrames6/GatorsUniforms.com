Gators Uniform Tracker is dedicated to tracking, covering, commentating, and breaking Florida Gators uniform news and history.

*Gators Uniform Tracker is not associated or affiliated with the University of Florida, the Florida Gators, or the UAA*

Gators Uniform Tracker is the largest collegiate uniform tracker on Twitter with over 8,000 followers. Beginning in July 2018, we have strived to provide the latest uniforms news while keeping a detailed history of Florida Gators football uniforms and other sports where applicable. Our work has been featured on sites such as UniWatch, 24/7 sports, and Saturday Down South. We consistently break uniform news including the 1960’s throwback football uniforms worn for the 2019 Auburn game. More information can be found @GatorsUnis on Twitter.
